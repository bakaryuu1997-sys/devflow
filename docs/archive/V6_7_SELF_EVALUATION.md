# v6.7 Self Evaluation

## What improved

The app now has a clear final approval step. It no longer stops at risk dashboards and completion gates; it can freeze a release approval snapshot and export a record suitable for review or portfolio evidence.

## What stayed intentionally small

- No new dependency.
- No PDF export yet.
- Approval record is Markdown, which is easy to copy, diff, test, and archive.

## Remaining limitation

The approval record does not yet compare multiple sign-offs or support post-release retrospective notes. That is a good candidate for v6.8.
